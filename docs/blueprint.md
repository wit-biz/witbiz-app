# **App Name**: WitCRM

## Core Features:

- Contact Management: Store and manage customer contact information.
- Lead Tracking: Track leads and their progress through the sales pipeline.
- Task Management: Create and assign tasks to team members, and track their completion.
- Intelligent Task Suggestions: Suggest the optimal next task for users based on activity and the opportunity, as a tool to make better business decisions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080) for a sense of professionalism and trust.
- Background color: Light teal (#E0F8F8) for a clean, calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft gold (#D4AF37) to highlight important elements and CTAs.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif for clean and modern readability.
- Use a set of consistent and professional icons.
- Maintain a clean, organized layout to promote clarity and focus.
- Incorporate subtle animations for feedback and to guide users.
